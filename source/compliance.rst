Regulatory compliance
=====================

Wazuh helps implement compliance requirements to provide regulatory compliance support and visibility by providing automation, improved security controls, log analysis and incident response. Out of the box, Wazuh provides support for PCI DSS, HIPAA, NIST 800-53, TSC and GDPR frameworks and standards. In addition to the default compliance frameworks and standards provided by Wazuh, it is possible to create monitor for custom compliance standards by adding the compliance identifier in the ``<group>`` tag.
